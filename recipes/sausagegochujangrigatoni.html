Sausage Gochujang Rigatoni

Sausage Gochujang Rigatoni

You need to try this!

Ingredients for 1

  • 3 Good quality sausages
  • Half a shallot
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 100ml of single cream
  • 2 crushed then chopped cloves of garlic
  • 120g of rigatoni pasta
  • 15g grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon of gochujang
  • 10g coriander
  • Juice of 1 lime

Method

  1. Peel the skin from the sausages then fry them in olive oil a pan on medium heat, breaking them up as you go.
  2. Deglaze the pan with a splash of water.
  3. When sausages are browned remove from the pan.
  4. Chop shallot and garlic and add to the pan along with your butter.
  5. Add your pasta to a pan of boiling water and cook til al dente, reserving a ladle of pasta water.
  6. Add gochujang and cream to the pan.
  7. Add the cheese, honey and lime juice.
  8. Re-add the sausage meat and mix together.
  9. Add salt and pepper.
  10. When the pasta is done, drain then add to the sauce pan. Use some pasta water if it needs loosening up.
  11. Add your coriander and stir into the pasta and sauce.
  12. Serve up and top with grated lime zest and more coriander.
