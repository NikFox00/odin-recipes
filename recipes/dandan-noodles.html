DanDan Noodles

No need for a takeaway, this is much better!

Ingredients for 1

  • 3 Good Quality Sausages
  • Packet of straight to wok udon noodles
  • 1 Teaspoon of Chinese 5 spice
  • 1 Tablespoon of dark soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon of hot water
  • 1 heaped tablespoon of tahini paste
  • 1 or 2 tablespoons of chilli oil
  • One pak choi separated into leaves and stalks
  • Spring onions for garnish

Method

  1. Peel the skin from the sausages then fry them in olive oil a pan on medium heat with the Chinese 5 spice and some salt and black pepper.
  2. When the sausage bits are crisp add the pak choi stalks and udon noodles and mix together.
  3. Make up the dandan sauce:
  4. Mix the soy sauce, hot water, tahini paste and chilli oil in a small bowl.
  5. Add the sauce and the pak choi leaves to the pan and mix together for another minute.
  6. Serve up and add chopped spring onnions to garnish.
