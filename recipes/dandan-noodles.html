DanDan Noodles

No need for a takeaway, this is much better!

Ingredients

  • 3 Good Quality Sausages
  • Packet of straight to wok udon noodles
  • 1 Teaspoon of Chinese 5 spice
  • 1 Tablesppon of dark soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon of hot water
  • 1 heaped tablespoon of tahini paste
  • 1 or 2 tablespoons of chilli oil
  • One pak choi separated into leaves and stem
  • Spring onions for garnish

Method

  1. Peel the skin from the sausages then fry them in a pan on medium heat, breaking them up as you go.
  2. Once they start to brown up, remove them from the pan.
  3. Turn the heat down then add salt, fennel, chilli flakes and garlic.
  4. Fry this off for a minute or so.
  5. Add your pasta to a pan of boiling water and cook til al dente.
  6. Add the white wine and use it to deglaze the pan.
  7. Re-add the sausage meat.
  8. Add the tinned tomatoes and turn the heat up.
  9. When the pasta has been cooking for about 6 minutes add a ladeful of pasta water to the sausage and tomato pan.
  10. Add some salt to the sauce.
  11. When the pasta is done, drain then add to the sauce pan. Use some pasta water if it needs loosening up.
  12. Mix then serve with a twist of black pepper and a large grating of parmesan or pecorino cheese.
